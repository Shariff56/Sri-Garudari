Overview

Board, medium, and structure.

SGEPS follows the State Board syllabus of Karnataka, ensuring a rigorous, structured, and locally relevant curriculum. Our academic program is carefully designed to foster critical thinking, problem-solving, and a deep understanding of core subjects, preparing students for both higher education and real-world challenges.

Instruction is delivered fully in English medium, catering to a vibrant and diverse community of learners. We place a strong emphasis on linguistic proficiency, ensuring our students can communicate effectively and confidently on a global stage while remaining deeply connected to their cultural roots.
