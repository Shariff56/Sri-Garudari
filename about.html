About the institution

Education as the holistic development of character, values, and skill.

Sri Garudadri English Public School has operated in Shidlaghatta since 2017, under Vidyasiri Education Trust (R).

Our mission

A nurturing, stimulating environment.

We believe that education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge but the holistic development of an individual's character, values, and skills — pursued with the active cooperation of parents and the wider community.

Academic Achievement

A structured, English-medium State Board curriculum with consistent assessment.

Creativity & Discipline

Balanced learning that gives structure and room for original thinking equal weight.

Leadership

Students are given real responsibility, not just titles, from an early age.

Lifelong Learning

Value-based education intended to outlast the classroom.

Our Community

A Thriving Student Body

Our students and dedicated staff build a strong, supportive environment that fosters both academic and extracurricular excellence.

It gives immense pleasure to welcome you to our school community. We believe that education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge but the holistic development of an individual's character, values, and skills.

Our mission is to provide a nurturing and stimulating environment where every child is encouraged to discover their potential, pursue excellence, and develop into responsible and compassionate citizens.

We strive to foster academic achievement, creativity, discipline, leadership, and lifelong learning through a balanced and value-based education. Our dedicated teachers work tirelessly to inspire, guide, and support each student in their journey towards success. With the active cooperation of parents and the community, we are committed to shaping confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals.

"We invite you to join us in our endeavor to create a vibrant learning community where every child can learn, grow, and excel.

With best wishes."
